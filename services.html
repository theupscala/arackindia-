Our Services

Advanced manufacturing capabilities with precision and quality

CNC Laser Sheet Cutting

High-precision laser cutting for various materials including MS, SS, and Aluminium with clean edges and tight tolerances.

  • Cutting thickness: Up to 25mm
  • Materials: MS, SS, Aluminium
  • Precision: ±0.1mm

CNC Press Brake / Bending

Advanced press brake technology for accurate bending and forming operations with consistent repeatability.

  • Capacity: Up to 200 tons
  • Bending length: Up to 4 meters
  • Accuracy: ±0.5°

CNC Shearing / Cutting

Precision shearing services for straight cuts in various materials with excellent edge quality.

  • Capacity: Up to 12mm thick
  • Length: Up to 6 meters
  • Clean, burr-free cuts

Hydraulic Iron Worker

Multi-functional ironworker for punching, notching, and shearing operations in one versatile machine.

  • Punching: Up to 25mm holes
  • Notching: Various angles
  • Quick tooling changes

Bandsaw Cutting

Precise cutting of structural materials and profiles with minimal material waste and clean finishes.

  • Round & square profiles
  • Up to 400mm diameter
  • Angular cutting capability

NC Bus Bar

Specialized bus bar processing including punching, bending, and cutting for electrical applications.

  • Copper & Aluminium bars
  • Custom hole patterns
  • Precise bending angles

Engraving / Marking

Laser engraving and marking services for permanent identification and branding on various materials.

  • Text & logo engraving
  • Serial number marking
  • High contrast marks

Robot Welding

Automated welding systems ensuring consistent quality, superior finish, and high productivity.

  • MIG/TIG welding
  • Repeatable quality
  • Complex geometries
